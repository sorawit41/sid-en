# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Industrial factory managers, energy auditors, and engineering teams. They use this system on site or in office settings to register equipment, track energy audits/inspections, calculate potential cost/energy/CO2 savings, and catalog replacements.

## Product Purpose
ENGINSPECT is a premium, responsive energy audit and inspection system designed to monitor equipment status, simulate payback periods for equipment replacements, and optimize energy and greenhouse gas (GHG) reductions across multiple factories.

## Brand Personality
Modern Industrial Tech. Professional, data-driven, and highly precise. Using high-contrast dark/light modes, IBM Plex Sans typography, clean visual hierarchy, and subtle color cues (green for savings, red/amber for issues) to command authority and expert trust.

## Anti-references
- Cliché SaaS "beige/cream" backgrounds that look washed-out in active work environments.
- Low-contrast gray text on tinted white.
- Over-decorated card grids and nested cards that obscure clean tabular data.
- Side-stripe colored borders on cards.
- Text overflow on narrow screen viewports.

## Design Principles
- **Data First, Noise Last**: Minimize visual clutter. Primary focus on numbers, equipment metrics, and clear actions.
- **Immediate Contextual Clarity**: Users must instantly know which factory, category, and time period is active.
- **Harmonious Status Semantics**: Use color functionally, not decoratively. Status indicators must be highly legible and contrast-compliant (>=4.5:1).
- **Flawless Responsiveness**: Dashboards and data tables must degrade elegantly on smaller screens without overflowing or clipping.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- WCAG AA Level compliance: contrast ratio >=4.5:1 for body text, >=3:1 for large text.
- Full support for dark and light modes.
- Respect prefers-reduced-motion settings using smooth, subtle transition fades instead of complex scale transforms.
